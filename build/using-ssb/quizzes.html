Discussions, Quizzes, and Sessions

Discussions, Quizzes, and Sessions are added through the Lesson view page:

To add Discussions, Quizzes or Sessions to a Lesson, click on the + Discussion/Quiz button and select the type you would like to add. Discussions and Quizzes are displayed below the Block content on each Lesson.

The icons displayed next to the button show the total number of Discussions, Quizzes and Sessions within the lesson. To quickly navigate to the Discussion/Quiz/Session section in the lesson, you can click on the respective icon.

Discussion and Quiz Blocks can be edited by clicking on them from the Lesson view, and are done through very similar processes to the Lesson Blocks themselves.
